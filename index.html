N.T.Rama Rao Jr. Wikipedia

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr. (born 20 May 1983), popularly known by the initialism Jr. NTR, is an Indian actor who primarily works in Telugu cinema.
He is one of the highest-paid actors in Telugu cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2012.
Referred to in the media as the "Man of Masses", he has appeared in over 30 films.
He is a recipient of several accolades including, two Filmfare Awards, two Nandi Awards, and four CineMAA Awards.
